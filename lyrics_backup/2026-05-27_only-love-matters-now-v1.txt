[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The porch light flickers, the fire is low
There’s nowhere else I’d rather go
Just trace my hand and pull me in
And let this midnight spark begin

[Verse 1]
We left the rush of town behind
To find a simple peace of mind
Your fingers trace across my chest
And in your arms I find my rest

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers fade into a steady glow
The quiet heat between us starts to grow
I see the open craving in your eyes
We’re stripping off the heavy day's disguise

[Chorus]
Let the hours fade, let the whole world bow
Cause darling, only love matters now
I want to hold you close and catch your breath
And stay right here until there's nothing left

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, only love, just you and me
Caught up in this late-night gravity

[Verse 2]
The open road is miles away
There's nothing else we need to say
I hear the rhythm of your heart
It’s tearing all my walls apart

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The moonlight spills across the window pane
It washes over us like silver rain
I feel the urgent craving in your touch
I never knew I'd want someone this much

[Chorus]
Let the hours fade, let the whole world bow
Cause darling, only love matters now
I want to hold you close and catch your breath
And stay right here until there's nothing left

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, only love, just you and me
Caught up in this late-night gravity

[Bridge]
The ticking clock has lost its steady sound
With every single breath we’re heaven-bound
I’ll give you everything I am inside
There is no single feeling I can hide
I’m yours completely, it’s Saeed you hear
Just whispering these promises in your ear

[Spoken]
(Come a little closer to me)
(Let’s make a permanent memory)

[Final Chorus]
Let the hours fade, let the whole world bow
Cause darling, only love matters now
I want to hold you close and catch your breath
And stay right here until there's nothing left
